This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and the choices available to merchants and their customers. The App is installed by Shopify merchants (“Merchants”) to collect Cash-on-Delivery (COD) orders and send order notifications. For data submitted by a Merchant’s shoppers, the Merchant is the data controller and we act as a data processor on the Merchant’s behalf.

1. Information we collect

CategoryExamplesWhy
Order / shopper details Name, phone number, email (optional), shipping address, items, order value To create the order in the Merchant’s Shopify admin and send order confirmations
Store information Shop domain, access tokens, app settings (form fields, button style, plan) To operate the App for the Merchant’s store
Fraud-prevention logs Phone, email, IP address of recent orders To enforce Merchant-configured anti-fraud limits. Automatically deleted after 30 days.
Messaging data WhatsApp number, message status, customer replies (confirm / cancel / address update) To send order confirmations and process customer replies

2. How we use information

We use the information only to provide the App’s functionality, namely to:

  • create and update COD orders in the Merchant’s Shopify store;
  • send WhatsApp order confirmations and process customer replies;
  • apply Merchant-configured upsells, discounts and anti-fraud rules;
  • forward conversion events to advertising platforms only when the Merchant configures a pixel (see §4);
  • manage the Merchant’s subscription plan and usage limits.

We do not sell personal information, and we do not use shopper data for our own advertising.

4. Advertising pixels & conversion tracking

If a Merchant configures one or more advertising pixels, the App sends conversion events (e.g. order value, hashed contact data) to the chosen platform(s) to measure ad performance. This is controlled entirely by the Merchant and is disabled by default.

5. Data retention

We keep store settings and messaging configuration for as long as the App is installed. Fraud-prevention order logs are automatically deleted after 30 days. When a Merchant uninstalls the App, we revoke stored access tokens immediately, and we erase all of the store’s data when Shopify sends the shop/redact request (about 48 hours after uninstall).

6. GDPR & data-subject requests

We honor Shopify’s mandatory privacy webhooks (customers/data_request, customers/redact, shop/redact). Shoppers who wish to access or delete their data should contact the Merchant (the data controller); the Merchant can then trigger these requests through Shopify, and we will action them promptly.

7. Security

  • All traffic is served over HTTPS/TLS.
  • Incoming Shopify webhooks are verified with HMAC signatures.
  • Access tokens are stored securely and refreshed using short-lived, expiring credentials.
  • Access to production data is restricted to authorized personnel.

8. International transfers

Data may be processed in countries other than where the Merchant or shopper is located. We rely on our providers’ safeguards for any such transfers.

9. Children

The App is not directed to individuals under 16 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
